Best Practices for the Continue as {Name} Button The Continue As {Name} button can be a great way to increase clicks and engagement. It lets the user see their profile picture and name on our Login button and can help make your app feel more personalized. As a result, it can also be confusing if the user isn't expecting to see their name and profile photo in this context.
